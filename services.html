Screening

Brief, informal checks that help determine whether speech and language therapy may be beneficial for your child. If further support is recommended, we will guide you toward a full assessment or offer suggestions for strategies you can use at home.

Assessment

A comprehensive assessment offers an in-depth understanding of your child’s speech, language, and communication profile. It highlights both strengths and areas of need, using observation, standardised tools, and collaboration with families and educators. The goal is to build a full picture of your child’s communication skills and identify a clear path forward for therapy. Every assessment includes clear recommendations and a personalised therapy plan.

Therapy

Therapy is available in both 1:1 and group formats, with sessions tailored to meet the unique needs of each child or young person. We offer 30, 45, or 60-minute sessions online (teletherapy), at home, or at a community location such as nursery or school.

Consultation & Advice

We offer support and advice to families, educators, and schools on strategies to support communication skills at home or in school.

Training & Workshops

We deliver workshops or training sessions (both in-person and online) to help school staff and professionals support children with speech, language, and communication needs (SLCN).

School-Based Services

We bring our expertise into schools and nurseries; offering on-site therapy, screenings, reports, and staff training to support communication development in the classroom and wider school environments.
