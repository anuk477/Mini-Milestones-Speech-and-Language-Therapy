Welcome to The London Children's Practice

The London Children's Practice is a thriving multidisciplinary practice, providing services to children, young people and families in London and internationally for over 15 years.

Our therapy is underpinned by our company values of Growth, Collaboration, Diversity and Care. We offer a variety of therapeutic services and assessments tailored to meet the needs of all service users.

We have a central clinic in Sloane Square, London offering assessment and therapy to children and young people. We work with mainstream and specialist schools offering services in tailored packages to support individual students and the school as a whole. We support a number of Local Authorities, providing packages of assessments and therapeutic input. To find out more, please get in touch.

Are you worried about your child's development?

Our Introductory Therapy Package offers an affordable starting point for therapy, with two initial sessions, including a comprehensive assessment and a feedback/target-setting session, all for just £220.
