Championing every child’s communication journey.

London Children’s Practice delivers multidisciplinary assessments and therapy designed to help children and young people thrive at home, in school, and in their communities.

Who We Are

The London Children’s Practice is a thriving multidisciplinary practice, providing services to children, young people, and families in London and internationally for over 15 years.

Our clinic in Sloane Square offers welcoming spaces for assessments and therapy, while our outreach teams partner with mainstream and specialist schools across the UK. We also collaborate with Local Authorities to design targeted intervention packages that address statutory requirements and deliver real impact for learners.

Everything we do is guided by our values of Growth, Collaboration, Diversity, and Care. These principles ensure that every interaction is shaped by compassion, evidence-based practice, and a shared commitment to helping every child reach their potential.

Our Approach

We believe lasting change comes from working as a team with the child, their family, educators, and other professionals. That is why our therapists:

  • Deliver joined-up assessments across Speech and Language Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Educational Psychology, and Creative Therapies.
  • Design bespoke therapy plans that fit around each child’s strengths, interests, and learning style.
  • Provide clear, actionable recommendations that empower adults to support communication and participation every day.
  • Share progress insights through regular reviews, ensuring everyone moves forward together.

Specialist Teams, Personal Support

From early intervention to complex diagnostic pathways, our specialists bring deep expertise and a warm, practical approach. Whether you are a parent, school, or partner organisation, we are here to listen, guide, and deliver the right support at the right time.
